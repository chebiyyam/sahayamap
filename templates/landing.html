The Problem
What went wrong in the 2024 Vijayawada floods
Thousands of families were affected — not just by the floods, but by the failure of coordination between those who needed help and those who could provide it.
🔄

Duplicated Relief Efforts

Multiple NGOs sent food to the same village while other areas received nothing for days.

🏚️

Unsafe Home Entry

Families entered structurally damaged homes without knowing the risk, causing preventable injuries.

🗺️

No Real-Time Visibility

Government officials had no live picture of where damage was worst and where help was needed most.

🚧

Administrative Silos

Government, NGOs, and individual samaritans operated independently with zero coordination.

The Solution
Three modules. One platform. Zero silos.
🗺️

Citizen Damage Reporting

Any citizen can report flood damage from their phone — house damage, road blockages, medical emergencies, people trapped — with GPS location and photo. Reports appear instantly on a live map visible to government and NGOs.

పౌరులు వారి ప్రాంతంలో నష్టాన్ని నివేదించవచ్చు
Works on 2G signal
🏠

Home Safety Checker

Before entering a flood-damaged home, anyone — including illiterate users — can answer 10 simple visual questions and receive an instant Green / Yellow / Red safety rating. Works completely offline.

ఇల్లు సురక్షితమా? లోపలికి వెళ్ళే ముందు తనిఖీ చేయండి
100% offline
🤝

Relief Coordination Dashboard

NGOs and government log what supplies they have and where they're going. The system automatically detects duplication — if two organizations are sending food to the same village, it flags them to redirect elsewhere.

Stops duplication
How It Works
Simple enough for anyone to use during a crisis
1

Citizen reports damage on their phone

Even on 2G signal — selects damage type, gets GPS location, optionally adds photo. Takes under 60 seconds.

2

Report appears instantly on the live map

Government officials and NGOs see a real-time picture of where damage is worst across all of AP.

3

NGOs log their relief supplies and destination

The system checks for duplication and alerts organizations if another team is already heading to the same area.

4

Help reaches the right place at the right time

No duplication, no gaps, no wasted resources. Every village gets what it needs.
